Facts About Zebras

There are three species of zebra:

  1. The plain's zebra
  2. Grevy's zebra
  3. Mountain zebras.

With the most common being the plain's zebra.

Where can you find zebras?

Zebras can be found in both the grasslands and woodlands of eastern and southern Africa.

They can be found roaming in Kenya, Ethiopia, Namibia, Angola and South Africa, however the precise country depends on the species of zebra. Due to the difference in environment between countries.
